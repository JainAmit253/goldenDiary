About Golden Diary

Golden Diary is a B2B platform designed for gold manufacturers, wholesalers, retailers & bullion dealers in india. Manufacturers, wholesalers, retailers, and bullioners are all connected by the platform and via using Geo fencing technology user can connect with the vendors around 5 kms for getting desired item.
